About Me

My name is Naima Bensassi, I am from Morocco. I have been in the US for 20 years, I am married with 3 beautiful kids. I have an MS of science in Chemistry. I am currently a relationship manager at Santander Bank where I comply with all federal regulations and work with clients to uncover all their financial needs almost every day.

My life experiences have taught me to multitask, have strong organization skills,financial analysis, and strong communication skills. I believe that with a strong team, anything is possible. I’m always motivated to be better than yesterday and consistently set personal goals.In addition I adapt quickly in new environments and enjoy taking on difficult challenges. We as humans are always constantly learning, so you have to adapt quickly if you want to catch up.
